Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Recording & Study Materials

StudyScribe uses Apple's Speech Recognition framework, which is generally very accurate for clear English speech. Accuracy may vary with heavy accents, background noise, or very fast speakers. You can always edit the transcript after recording.

After recording, StudyScribe's AI analyses your lecture transcript and generates flashcards with key terms and definitions. Swipe through them to revise. Pro users also get spaced repetition to optimise review timing.

StudyScribe generates multiple choice and short answer questions based on the content of your lecture. Questions are designed to test comprehension, not just recall. Your scores are tracked so you can see where you need more revision.

Subscription & Billing

The free version includes:

  • 3 lectures per month
  • Full transcription
  • AI-structured notes
  • PDF export

StudyScribe Pro unlocks:

  • Unlimited lectures
  • Flashcard generation
  • Quiz mode with scoring
  • Spaced repetition
  • Full-text search across all notes
  • PDF export
  • 7-day free trial to try everything

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Open iPhone Settings
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find StudyScribe and tap Cancel Subscription

You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

Open StudyScribe on your new device and go to Settings. Tap "Restore Purchases" and your Pro subscription will be restored automatically, as long as you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

Privacy & Data

Open any note and tap the share button. You can export as PDF. Share to any app on your device: Notes, Files, email, AirDrop, or anywhere else.

You can delete individual recordings by swiping left on them in the notes list, or use the edit and delete buttons within any note. To delete all data, remove the app from your device. Since we don't store any of your data on our servers, deletion is immediate and complete.

Most universities allow recording of lectures for personal study purposes. However, policies vary by institution and jurisdiction. We recommend checking your university's recording policy and informing lecturers when possible. StudyScribe is intended for personal revision, not redistribution.
